Who We Serve
Care Settings We Support
From group homes to long-term care, we place compassionate professionals where they're needed most.

Group Homes

Supporting individuals with developmental disabilities in residential group settings with DSW, RSW, and support worker placements.

Long-Term Care Homes

Staffing PSWs, RPNs, and RNs to maintain safe care ratios in long-term care environments across Ontario.

Developmental Services

Experienced DSWs and behaviour technicians for agencies supporting adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities.

Respite Care

Qualified respite workers for families who need short-term relief support for loved ones with complex care needs.

Retirement Residences

Reliable, compassionate staff to support resident programming, personal care, and daily living in retirement communities.

Our Services
What Resalire Offers
Whether you're a facility needing coverage tonight or a healthcare worker seeking flexible shifts, we have you covered.

Relief & Temporary Staffing

Short-notice and scheduled relief coverage for sick calls, vacations, and unfilled shifts — keeping your care ratios intact.

Flexible Shift Matching

Healthcare professionals choose shifts that fit their schedules. Resalire matches skills to the right placement every time.

Pre-Screened Professionals

Every worker is background-checked, credential-verified, and trained before their first placement with us.

24/7 Coordinator Support

Our staffing coordinators are available around the clock to respond to last-minute needs and urgent coverage requests.

Training & Compliance

We ensure all staff meet sector-specific compliance requirements including WHMIS, First Aid, and safe handling certifications.

Long-Term Placement

Beyond relief, we support facilities in identifying reliable candidates for long-term or permanent roles.
